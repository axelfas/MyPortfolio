About Me

I am a 22-year-old Venezuelan UI/UX designer and front-end developer, currently residing in Valencia, Spain. With a solid education and experience in the design field, I am passionate about creating engaging and digital experiences.

My primary focus lies in designing intuitive and visually appealing interfaces while ensuring a seamless and optimized user experience. In addition to my UI/UX design expertise, I have also worked as a graphic designer, which has provided me with a comprehensive understanding of visual design and a unique perspective in approaching projects from different angles.
